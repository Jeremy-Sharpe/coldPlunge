Our Story

Founded in 2022, Platypus Plunge was born from a passion for wellness and recovery. Our mission is to make the benefits of cold therapy accessible to everyone through innovative, efficient, and user-friendly cold plunge solutions.

What started as a small team of health enthusiasts has grown into a leading provider of premium cold plunge systems, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Malaysia.
